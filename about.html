Who I Am

Hi! I'm Alex Johnson, a full-stack web developer with over 5 years of experience crafting modern, responsive, and performant websites. I specialize in front-end development and love turning complex ideas into beautiful, functional digital experiences.

I believe great development is about more than just writing code – it's about understanding the user, collaborating with teams, and delivering results that matter.
